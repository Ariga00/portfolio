About Me

Hello! I'm Ariga, a student at The Cooperative University of Kenya, pursuing a Diploma in Cybersecurity. My journey in tech began with a curiosity about how things work, starting from my primary education at St Mary's Mosocho, then high school at Oriwo Boys High School. I later attended Modcom School of Institute for software development, where I learned some foundational skills but developed a strong interest in cybersecurity and web development.

I'm passionate about blending technology with creativity, and when I'm not studying or coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, enjoying a good cup of coffee, or diving into cybersecurity challenges. I believe in continuous learning and staying updated with the latest trends in web development and cybersecurity.
